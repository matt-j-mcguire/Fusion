
Connection strings will be in the format: 
Driver.CommPoint-IOpoint = OptoMux.101-1


Standard prefixes for possible future
---------------------------
OptoMux			Standard DK Opto Driver
ArduinoMegaDK	specially designed driver to work with a DK designed software on a Arduino Mega
Modbus			Standard Modbus driver
MilleniumDKxx	specially designed driver to work with Millenium with a special program loaded

connection format standards
--------------------------------------
Prefixes: may contain letters or numbers no symbols [a-z,A-Z,0-9]
Identifier: may contain letters or numbers no symbols [a-z,A-Z,0-9]
Point: may contain letters or numbers no symbols [a-z,A-Z,0-9]
